Origin

Treasure(previously Treasure-13) is a 12-membered boygroup from South Korea through a survival show named YG TREASURE BOX. Treasure is from one of the top 4 enterainment agencies in he country known for artists like BIGBANG, BLACKPINK,AKMU,WINNER,IKON etc. The group consists of: Choi Hyunsuk, Jihoon, Yoshi, Junkyu, Mashiho, Yoon Jaehyuk, Asahi, Bang Yedam, Haruto, Doyoung, Park Jeongwoo, and So Junghwan. The group debuted on August 7th, 2020. After debuting in 2020 with their single album, The First Step: Chapter One, their tetralogy sold over one million copies five months into their career.
